# Commercial & Organizational Licensing

Bounce-CTI is **source-available** under a custom license (see
[LICENSE](./LICENSE)) that permits free personal, non-commercial use
by individuals.

**Any other use requires a separate written license agreement.** This
includes — but is not limited to — the following scenarios:

## Who needs a license

- **Companies & MSSPs** — using Bounce-CTI for internal SOC operations,
  client-facing investigations, or as part of a paid service offering.
- **Government & public sector** — CERTs, defense agencies, law
  enforcement, regulators, ministries.
- **Healthcare & critical infrastructure** — hospitals, energy
  operators, transportation, water utilities.
- **Educational & research institutions** — universities, labs,
  publicly-funded research programs.
- **NGOs & charities** — humanitarian, environmental, advocacy
  organizations.
- **Integrators & resellers** — anyone packaging, embedding, or
  distributing Bounce-CTI as part of another product or service.
- **Hosting / SaaS providers** — anyone offering Bounce-CTI (or a
  derivative) as a hosted or managed service.

If you are unsure whether your use case requires a license, please
ask. I will respond honestly, including telling you when no license
is needed.

## What a license typically includes

Each license is negotiated individually. Typical scope covers:

- The right to deploy and run Bounce-CTI within a defined organizational
  perimeter (number of users, sites, environments).
- The right to integrate Bounce-CTI with internal tooling.
- Optional: support, customization, integration help, training.
- Optional: feature work on a contract basis.
- Optional: priority access to new releases.

Licensing terms (duration, fee, scope) depend on use case, organization
size, and intended deployment.

## How to request a license

Send an email with the following information:

- **Organization** — name, type (company / government / NGO / research / …),
  approximate size.
- **Intended use** — internal SOC, client investigations, integration,
  research, training, …
- **Deployment scope** — number of users / analysts, on-premise vs.
  cloud, regions.
- **Timeline** — when do you want to start, target duration.
- **Contact** — name, role, email, phone (optional).
